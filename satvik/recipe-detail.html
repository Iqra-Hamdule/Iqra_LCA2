Moong Dal Chilla

A light, protein-rich, and Satvik breakfast option ‚Äî easy to digest and packed with natural goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow moong dal (soaked for 3-4 hours)
  • 1-inch ginger piece
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste (sendha namak for strict Satvik)
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Drain the soaked moong dal and blend it with ginger and green chili into a smooth batter.
  2. Add turmeric, salt, and chopped coriander. Mix well.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or iron tawa. Spread a ladle of batter like a pancake.
  4. Drizzle a little ghee around the edges and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve warm with coriander chutney or homemade dahi (yogurt).
